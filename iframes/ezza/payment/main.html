What is CVV2?

The CVV2 is the three-digit number printed in the signature space on the back of most credit cards, such as Visa, Mastercard, and Discover cards. Discover Card calls it the "Cardmember ID".

The CVV2 number is always the last group of numbers in the signature space on the back of the card. It is not part of your regular credit card number.
